Lupo Support

Lupo is a habit tracker where you raise a wolf by keeping your real habits. Here is how to get help.

Contact

Email chleb@chlebholdings.com and we will get back to you.

My progress disappeared

Your wolf and streak are stored on your own device. They can be lost if you clear your browser data, switch devices, or remove the app. If you reinstalled, open the app the same way you did before (same browser or the same home screen icon).

The app looks out of date

If you added Lupo to your home screen, delete it and add it again once. After that it updates itself.

Reminders are not showing

On iPhone, reminders need the app added to your home screen first. Open it from there, then turn the reminder on in Profile.

Reset everything

Open Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap Reset All Progress. This wipes your wolf and all stored data from your device.
