# Lupo Monetization Plan

Researched against Finch, Forest, Habitica, Fabulous, and Duolingo (2024-2025 models).

## Model
Freemium subscription, subscription-led, with a **generous free-forever core** and **one** optional premium tier ("Lupo Pack"). Opt-in rewarded ads only, free tier only, as a retention/conversion lever, never a primary revenue line. No sold in-app currency (keeps the discipline brand clean, avoids pay-to-win). The native iOS "Verified Mode" (real Screen Time auto-verification) is the headline paid feature once iOS ships.

## Pricing (USD)
- **Yearly: $49.99/yr** — the hero. Show this first. ~$4.17/mo, undercuts Finch's $69.99 to win the challenger conversion. Price-test toward $59.99 once Verified Mode ships.
- **Monthly: $9.99/mo** — the decoy/anchor. Do not promote. Category parity with Finch, below Duolingo's $12.99.
- **Lifetime: $89.99 one-time** — hidden. Only show in Settings and as a win-back offer in the cancel flow (turns churners into one payment without cannibalizing subs). Pull it if it exceeds ~15-20% of new paid conversions.
- **14-day free trial** fired after the user's first 3-day streak (real attachment), re-surfaced at the onboarding emotional peak.

## Free tier (must feel like winning, not a demo)
Entire wolf-raising loop, all 5 art stages and all levels (never gate evolution), manual screen-time logging, the optional habits, full streak system + milestone celebrations, the forgiveness system (3 free streak freezes/month + rest day), basic stats, an earnable rotating subset of cosmetics, shareable milestone cards. The disciplined free user is the @raiselupo UGC engine that drives installs.

## Premium ("Lupo Pack")
- **Verified Mode** (iOS) — Apple Screen Time auto-verification. The headline iOS reason-to-download.
- **Unlimited streak freezes** (vs 3/month free) — the direct loss-aversion conversion lever.
- **Ad-free.**
- **Full cosmetic wardrobe** (pelts/auras/collars/environments/howls) vs the free rotating subset.
- **Advanced insights** (weekly report, extended history, habit-vs-streak correlations).
- Early access + extra seasonal cosmetics; later: multiple wolves, pack/social flair.

## Ads
Opt-in **rewarded video only, free tier only.** Flagship placement: **streak-rescue** — "Watch a 30s ad to give [wolf] a grace day" once the 3 free freezes are used. This monetizes loss-aversion, softens the gate so beginners don't churn, and itself advertises Pack ("tired of ads? unlimited freezes are in the Pack"). Hard rules: zero interstitials, zero banners, nothing mid-celebration or on the wolf home screen. Mediation: AppLovin MAX primary, AdMob + Meta as demand. Realistically ads are a rounding error at small scale; their real job is retention + conversion.

## Launch order
1. **Now (PWA, no paywall, no ads):** ship the complete free loop, prove D7/D30 retention. Run the referral waitlist as the iOS launch funnel.
2. **After retention proves out:** turn on the soft paywall as above (annual-first, monthly decoy, lifetime hidden).
3. **Alongside 2:** opt-in rewarded ads for free users, streak-rescue first.
4. **Native iOS:** ship Verified Mode as the flagship paid upgrade. Start the Apple Family Controls entitlement request early (1-5 week lead time, gates everything).

Do not gate any core feature at launch. Finch built ~$30M ARR on a fully-functional free core.

## Revenue reality
Subscriptions are the revenue; ads monetize the ~90%+ who never pay. At a realistic 3-6% paid conversion and ~$40 net/paying-user/yr after Apple's cut: 10k MAU x 4% x $40 = ~$16k/yr; it gets interesting past ~50-100k MAU (~$80k-$160k/yr). So **install volume (UGC + waitlist) is the real revenue driver** — monetization math is downstream of reach. Defend the ~35% month-1 churn with an immediate post-purchase reward moment and streak-loss re-engagement.
