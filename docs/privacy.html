Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2, 2026

Lupo is a habit tracker made by Chleb Holdings LLC. This policy explains what we collect and how we handle it. We kept it short because there is not much to it.

What we store

Your habits, your wolf, your streak, your settings, and any art you upload are stored only on your own device in your browser storage. We do not have a server that holds your tracking data and we cannot see it.

The waitlist

If you choose to join the waitlist, the email address you type is sent to us so we can tell you when the full app launches. We use it for that and nothing else. We do not sell it or share it.

Screen Time

On the web version you log your own numbers by hand. A future native iPhone app may read your real Screen Time through Apple Family Controls, with your permission, and that data stays on your device.

Analytics and tracking

The app does not run third party advertising or tracking pixels. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may keep standard server logs such as IP addresses for security and reliability.

Children

Lupo is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Your choices

You can wipe everything at any time from Profile, Reset All Progress, or by clearing the site data in your browser. That removes your wolf and all stored data from your device.

Contact

Questions about this policy go to chleb@chlebholdings.com.
